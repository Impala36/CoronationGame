Facts & Questions

These are some facts & questions that may help to clear your doubts.

What is JLPT?

Japanese Language Proficiency Test (JLPT) from the Japan Foundation is an examination used to measure and certify the proficiency of those whose native language is not Japanese. 

What is the payment procedure like?

Payment is not required upon enrolment, and will be requested through WhatsApp, Telegram or email.

For Online classes: Payment of $200 will be requested when you wish to proceed to the 2nd lesson.
For Physical classes: Payment of $300 will be requested close to the course start date (19th April 2021).
For 1 to 1 Online classes: Payment of $300 will be requested when you wish to proceed to the 2nd lesson.
For 1 to 1 Physical classes: Payment of $400 will be requested before commencing the 1 to 1.

Our administrator will contact you to confirm your schedule and will send the PayNow QR code and Google Pay spot code for your payment.

Is the address only for the rented classroom?

Yes. Currently, we do not have a physical office, and all employees work from home unless they have a physical lesson at the rented classroom.

How do I get the free trial online lesson?

All online lessons begin with the first lesson being a free trial.
Request for payment only begins when you wish to continue with the second lesson.

How do I get the $50 referral reward?

It will be transferred to you via PayNow or Google Pay, after confirmation of your friend's payment for his/her enrolment.
This reward applies only to current or previous students of our school.
