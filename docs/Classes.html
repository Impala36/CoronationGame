Our Classes

Online

We use Discord to communicate with students, Google Slides to teach and Jamboard worksheets for classwork. Conversations between students occur in private groups with the teacher dropping by.

Physical Classroom

The classic classroom where slides are projected on the whiteboard, Jamboard worksheets are printed out and conversations between students can take place.

1 to 1 Online / Physical 

You can enjoy a course with the full guidance of a teacher at the comfort of your own home, at a suitable public setting or at the teacher's place.
