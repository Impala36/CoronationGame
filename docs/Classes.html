Our Classes

Online

We use Discord to communicate with students, Google Slides to teach and Jamboard worksheets for classwork. Conversations between students occur in private groups.

Payment of $200 will be requested when you wish to proceed to the 2nd lesson.
Note: Classes may be grouped or postponed, so we will contact you if there's any changes.

We offer a Free Trial Lesson for online classes! 

Current students can get a $50 referral reward when they refer a friend who has paid for a course!

Physical Classroom

The classic classroom where slides are projected on the whiteboard, Jamboard worksheets are printed out and conversations between students can take place.

Payment of $300 will be requested close to the course start date.
Note: Classes may be grouped or postponed, so we will contact you if there's any changes.

Current students can get a $50 referral reward when they refer a friend who has paid for a course!

1 to 1 Online / Physical

We offer a Free Trial Lesson for online classes! 

1 to 1 sessions can be arranged at any time.
