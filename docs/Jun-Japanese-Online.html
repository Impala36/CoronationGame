Unique Teaching Style

  • Our students' interest is our top priority, so we want to retain their Japanese knowledge in the most effective ways.
  • By providing vibrant, informative and easy-to-learn slides and worksheets, we make textbooks a thing of the past.
  • Conversational practice between students occur every lesson to keep everyone's speaking capabilities afresh.

Full recap of lessons, every lesson

Before the start of the next lesson, there will be a quick revision of previously taught lessons, so that you don’t forget the important points needed to understand the next topic.

Flashcards infused

Vocabulary is a very important aspect on any language, and Flashcards is a proven way to teach them effectively. Our slides will teach vocabulary the same way Flashcards do.

Homework is optional!

We see homework as an optional, not a requirement. Homework will be for students who want to do some revision throughout the week or want more practice.
