Game Description

Coronation is a multiplayer medieval life simulation indie game for PC. In Coronation, you are free to build castles, towns or villages, manage families and their lives around in an online open world. The game features a building system that is easy to use but also powerful for advanced builders, AI system with work automations and a unique combat system. Players can explore a world full of events, players and people to meet and ways to keep everyone safe, and live happy, prosperous lives.


Studio Description

Twin Sword Studio is an indie company founded by Mbdot and Impala. The studio’s only focus is to build the ultimate medieval life simulation game. The company currently has 2 members and is funded by patrons at Patreon.


Team

Mbdot (Game Designer)
Mbdot was a ​Building Information Modeling (BIM) Specialist who created construction drawings through 3D models. Immediately after having enough fun with Mount & Blade: Bannerlord, Mbdot started working on Coronation, the dream game formulated in mind due to the experience of playing many fun games. With some prior knowledge in 3D modelling, Mbdot picked up Blender and Unreal Engine with the help of the saints in the Internet.

Impala (Game Programmer)
Impala wrote his first text-based game in Python when he was 14, inspired by RuneScape. Unfortunately, he could not go for a game development course due to financial woes, and had to take a scholarship in Precision Engineering, which paid for all the school fees. While working full-time as a BIM Specialist to complete the scholarship's 3-year bond, Impala studied part-time for a degree in Software Engineering. Equipped with professional software skills and having worked on 3 small Unity games made during his free time, Impala switched over to Unreal Engine with the guidance of YouTube tutorials.
