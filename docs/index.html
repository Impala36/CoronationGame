A multiplayer open-world medieval life simulator

Create your own Kingdom

Feel like an actual King or Queen of your own empire with real lineages, dine and celebrate together with life-like people, manage a player-driven economy, and fight alongside comrades that you personally know in realistic battles and sieges.

Live the Medieval life

Find a medieval job, rent a place to live, and take care life's needs such as Hunger, Thirst, Energy, Call of Nature, Cleanliness, Social, Religion. (WIP)

Be a Master Architect

There is a wide variety of building parts and functional furniture that you can decorate your interiors and exteriors.

Work, Train and Fight

Level up your skills, engage in combat at a dungeon, train a formidable army and equip them with powerful weapons.
